How can I report a lost or found item?


To report a lost item, click on the "Lost" tab in the navigation bar and fill out the form. To report a found item, click on the "Found" tab in the navigation bar and fill out the form.

Where can I see the status of my lost items?


Any found items are listed on the homepage and the found page (accessed by clicking the "Found" tab on the navigation bar). If an item is yours, come to our office located in Hancock to pick up your item.

What does "Admin Login" mean and why can't I log in?


Admin Login is just for the administrators of the system. In order to create an account, you must be an administrator.
